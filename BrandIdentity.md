# Brand Identity Guidelines

## Brand Name
RaamBow TechSolutions

---

## Tagline
Modern solutions. Clean execution.

---

## Brand Personality
Modern  
Minimal  
Professional  
Innovative  
Reliable  

---

## Brand Voice
Clear and direct communication.

Confident but not aggressive.

Professional and minimal.

Example:
We build fast and clean solutions.  
We focus on clarity and performance.  

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
Dark base color for background.

Accent color (blue or purple).

---

### Secondary Colors
Soft supporting tones.

Used for highlights and sections.

---

### Neutral Colors
White for backgrounds.

Gray for text and UI elements.

---

## Typography

### Font Family
Poppins  
Inter  

---

### Hierarchy
Headings: Bold  
Subheadings: Medium  
Body: Regular  

---

## Logo Guidelines

### Clear Space
Maintain spacing around logo.

---

### Minimum Size
Ensure readability on all devices.

---

### Do’s
Use on clean backgrounds.

Maintain proportions.

---

### Don’ts
Do not stretch.

Do not distort.

Do not add effects.

---

## UI Design System

### Buttons
Primary buttons with solid fill.

Secondary buttons with outline.

Hover effects with slight animation.

---

### Cards
Rounded corners.

Soft shadows.

Clean layout.

---

### Inputs
Clear labels.

Focus highlight.

Accessible design.

---

## Animations
Use smooth transitions (200–300ms).

Avoid excessive motion.

Keep interactions subtle.

---

## Visual Style
Clean layouts.

Balanced spacing.

Minimal design elements.

Focus on clarity.

---

## Usage Principles
Maintain consistency.

Avoid clutter.

Focus on usability.

Ensure readability.

---

## Responsiveness
Design for mobile-first.

Ensure adaptability across screens.

Maintain usability on all devices.

---

## Final Note
Consistency and simplicity define the brand.

Every design decision should support clarity and usability.
